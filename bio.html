About Bri Jones

Bri Jones was born and raised in Farrell, Pennsylvania, then later relocated to the Washington D.C. area with her young son. Bri graduated from Slippery Rock University with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting. Bri completed her Master's in Business Administration at University of Maryland University College.

When Bri isn't tied to a computer crunching numbers, she enjoys writing short stories and sharing them with her family and friends. Bri also enjoys traveling and spending time with her son. Bri likes to explore and learn about history and different cultures. Bri feels that everyone has hidden talents that are waiting to be explored.



Message from the Author

I was encouraged to write this tale of Onika, to show a positive image that focuses on people of color. This is a positive and uplifting story, which young & mature people of color can be proud of. When you look at the media, cartoons and society that our culture is exposed to you donâ€™t see the strong, secure and powerful people of color doing well or displayed in a positive image. I wanted to plant that seed, that we are all Kings & Queens. We are a culture of fashion, class, respect, strength and of importance.

The message I feel that comes from this story is everyone is faced with different challenges but forgiveness is the path to peace. Time lost apart is not worth it, time together is so much more valuable.

Regardless of your age; this is a story that will captivate the emotions and imaginations of the whole family.
