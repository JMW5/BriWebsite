Summary

Onika is the Princess of a beautiful kingdom in Africa and is eight months away from her arranged marriage ceremony. Life as Onika once knew it, is about to change after she meets a Prince at her kingdom's annual festival. Onika is faced with a decision: to honor the promise she made to her parents and kingdom or disgrace them by following her heart. Onika doesn't want to bring shame to her family and kingdom, but she will soon find out the consequences of either decision are more serious than what she expected.
